Why Naruto is the best anime!

Derek Viera
Oct 23, 2022

Naruto is the perfect protagonis, in the one of the most well-written and animated shows. The story of Naruto Uzumaki is amazing and quite emotional.
Naruto grows up with the help of many friends and masters in order to grow up to be a great shinobi. Naruto is able to build bonds with many people
and learn so much from than in order to face many challenges and strong enemies.

Why One Piece is a great anime!

Derek Viera
Oct 23, 2022

One Piece is based on adventures with the main character Monkey D. Luffy and his crews in a world of pirates. Luffy goal throughout the show is to
set sail and search for the treasure called the One Piece. They are "Devil Fruits" in this world which grants people who eat them superpowers.
There are many amazing fight scenes between these users with superpowers making it a great anime!
